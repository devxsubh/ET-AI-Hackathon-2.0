# SOP — Impeller Replacement v3.2
**Equipment:** Kirloskar KDS-550 / Pump P-101 class  
**Authored by:** Ramesh Kumar  
**Reviewed by:** Anand Mehta, Plant Manager  
**Effective:** April 2019

## Purpose
Safe removal and installation of impeller and mechanical seal on Unit 3 feed water pumps.

## Prerequisites
- LOTO completed
- Mechanical Seal Type-A kit available
- KDS-550 Impeller in stores
- SKF Bearing 6205 if drive-end work is scheduled

## Steps
1. Drain casing and isolate suction/discharge valves (Control Valve V-44 upstream).
2. Remove coupling insert and housing.
3. Extract impeller; inspect for cavitation pitting.
4. Install new impeller; torque per OEM manual XR-22 (Kirloskar KDS-550).
5. Fit Mechanical Seal Type-A per manufacturer gap specs.
6. Reassemble, prime, and bump-test under supervision of Sr. Maintenance Engineer.

## Related incidents
Seal failure March 2019 (Pump P-101) — cavitation root cause.
