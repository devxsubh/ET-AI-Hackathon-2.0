# Maintenance Report #2341
**Bharat Engineering Works, Pune — Unit 3 (Cooling Water Circuit)**  
**Asset:** Pump P-101 (Kirloskar KDS-550 centrifugal feed water pump)  
**Date:** 14 March 2019  
**Author:** Ramesh Kumar, Sr. Maintenance Engineer  
**Reviewed by:** Anand Mehta, Plant Manager

## Incident
Mechanical seal failure on Pump P-101 during night shift. Operators reported unusual vibration, then seal leakage into the drip tray.

## Root cause
Cavitation due to partially clogged suction strainer and low NPSH during monsoon load swing. Secondary damage to impeller vanes.

## Resolution
1. Isolated P-101 and locked out.
2. Replaced impeller (KDS-550 Impeller).
3. Upgraded to Mechanical Seal Type-A.
4. Replaced SKF Bearing 6205 on drive end as precaution.
5. Cleaned suction strainer element.

## Outcome
Pump returned to service 16 March 2019. Ramesh Kumar authored follow-up SOP for impeller replacement.

## Parts used
- SKF Bearing 6205
- Mechanical Seal Type-A
- KDS-550 Impeller
- O-ring set (Viton)
- Gasket kit P-101
