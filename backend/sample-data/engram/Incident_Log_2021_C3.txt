# Incident Log — Compressor C-3
**Date:** 9 August 2021  
**Asset:** Compressor C-3  
**Author / Fixed by:** Ramesh Kumar  

## Event
Drive-end bearing seizure on Compressor C-3. High temperature alarm then shaft lock.

## Cause
Lubrication starvation; same SKF Bearing 6205 family as used on Pump P-101.

## Actions
- Replaced SKF Bearing 6205
- Replaced Coupling insert C-3
- Documented Bearing change-out procedure for C-3 class compressors

## Notes
Ramesh is the only engineer with hands-on experience of this 2021 failure on C-3.
